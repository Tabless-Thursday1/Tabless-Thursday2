Tabless Thursday

Would like to welcome you to the rest of your life! We have all been there. Tabs can multiple like rabbits and it takes no time at all. Afraid to close your browser but your computer is getting sluggish. Taking longer and longer to switch between pages. Other applications don't want to start or wake up. Your pc fan just hit overdrive and the hum no has you on the edge of your seat.

Green App

That fan kicking into overdrive? Have 16 million different tabs open and your starting to feel that lag? Keep your tabs organized! Tabless Thursday allows you to put your favorite sites into categories so you can easily re-visit them when you need to. Find a new site but don't have time to look it over? Place it in the for 'For Later', or the 'Got ta get to' sections and never loose it again! Keeping your tabs organized keeps them out of your ram. Your computer doesn't have to keep track of them. Keeping them out of memory and using less processing power. Leaving your machine a lean and powerful beast.

Organize

Organize your tabs! You can put your favorite tabs into different catagories and make it easier to find them. It is time to get organized. Put them into a category, you create, to clear the clutter, making it easier to find what your looking for and easier to share with friends! Doing a research paper? Use Tabless Thursday to get all your research organized in one place. Love antique cars? You can have one category for Hudson and one category for Morgans!
